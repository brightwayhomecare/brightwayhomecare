## Copywriting Guidance
✅ Keep sentences short and clear
✅ Use respectful tone
✅ Avoid medical promises
✅ For medication mention: use "as permitted"

Examples:
- ✅ "Medication reminders/support (as permitted)"
- ❌ "We provide hospital-level nursing care"
