# Brightway Home Care LLC — Theme Guide

## Brand Colors
Primary palette:
- Blue 800 (Primary): Headings, buttons, top navigation, footer
- Teal 300 (Accent): Icons, section dividers, highlights
- White (Base): Background, clean spacing

Suggested usage:
- Main background: White
- Section headers: Blue 800
- Icons + callouts: Teal 300
- CTA buttons: Blue 800 with white text

---

## Typography
Recommended fonts:
- Headings: Poppins / Montserrat (SemiBold)
- Body text: Inter / Open Sans (Regular)

Font sizing:
- H1: 44–54px
- H2: 28–36px
- H3: 18–22px
- Body: 16–18px
- Small: 12–14px

---

## UI Style
- Clean and modern
- Rounded corners: 16px–24px
- Soft shadows only (minimal)
- Plenty of white space
- Large, readable text (especially on mobile)

---

## Components
### Primary Button
- Background: Blue 800
- Text: White
- Example labels:
  - Call / WhatsApp
  - Contact Us

### Secondary Button
- Background: White
- Border: Blue 800
- Text: Blue 800

### Info Cards
- White background
- Teal icon on top/left
- Blue title
- Short supporting text

---

## Photography
Preferred photos:
- Home exterior (bright daylight)
- Clean living room
- Caregiver supporting a senior (professional)

Avoid:
- Crowded images
- Low-quality photos
- Resident faces without consent

---

## Tone of Voice
- Calm, respectful, professional
- Trust-building language
- Simple and clear
- Avoid medical claims

Good examples:
- "Safe and supportive home environment"
- "24/7 supervision and care"
- "Schedule a tour today"
